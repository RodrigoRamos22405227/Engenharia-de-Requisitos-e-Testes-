# Vision

The system supports the Intake & Discovery slice by enabling structured data capture, validation, and evidence consistency checks while strictly enforcing GDPR-aligned data minimization and retention principles. It ensures that only necessary personal data is collected, processed, and retained, allowing the organization to determine whether a case is “Ready to Proceed” or “Need More Information” in a compliant and controlled manner.

## Objectives

1. Ensure personal data is collected only when strictly necessary (data minimization principle).
2. Validate completeness and internal consistency of submitted information and evidence.
3. Support a clear and auditable decision outcome: “Ready to Proceed” vs “Need More Data”.

## Non-Objectives

1. The system will not implement full automated legal compliance assessment.
2. The system will not define external legal policies beyond the intake slice.
3. The system will not manage long-term archival storage outside defined retention constraints.
