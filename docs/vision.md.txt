# Vision 

The system supports the Intake & Discovery slice by enabling structured data capture, validation, and evidence consistency checks while strictly enforcing GDPR-aligned data minimization and retention principles. It ensures that only necessary personal data is collected, processed, and retained, allowing the organization to determine whether a case is “Ready to Proceed” or “Need More Information” in a compliant and controlled manner.

- Variant number: 9
- Primary persona: Privacy Officer / Legal
- Key constraint focus: GDPR-style retention and minimization
- Mandatory slice: Intake & Discovery

## Objectives
Ensure personal data is collected only when strictly necessary (data minimization principle).
Validate completeness and internal consistency of submitted information and evidence.
Support a clear and auditable decision outcome: “Ready to Proceed” vs “Need More Data”.

## Non-Objectives
The system will not implement full automated legal compliance assessment.
The system will not define external legal policies beyond the intake slice.
The system will not manage long-term archival storage outside defined retention constraints.
