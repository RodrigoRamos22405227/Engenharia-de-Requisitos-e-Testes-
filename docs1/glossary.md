# Glossary

**Intake** – The initial phase where user information and supporting evidence are collected in a structured manner.

**Discovery** – The process of identifying missing, inconsistent, or unclear information during intake.

**Persona** – A defined stakeholder role guiding the system’s requirements (e.g., Privacy Officer).

**Data Minimization** – GDPR principle requiring collection of only strictly necessary personal data.

**Retention Policy** – Rules that define how long personal data may be stored before deletion.

**Personal Data** – Any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.

**Validation** – The process of verifying that submitted data is complete and correctly formatted.

**Consistency Check** – Verification that different pieces of submitted information do not contradict each other.

**Evidence** – Supporting documentation provided to justify or validate submitted information.

**Decision Outcome** – The system result after evaluation (e.g., “Ready to Proceed” or “Need More Data”).

**Requirement** – A documented need or constraint that the system must satisfy.

**Acceptance Criteria** – Specific conditions that must be met for a requirement to be considered fulfilled.

**Test Case** – A structured procedure used to verify that a requirement behaves as expected.

**Traceability** – The ability to link requirements to test cases and validation evidence.

**Constraint** – A limitation or rule that restricts system behavior (e.g., GDPR compliance rules).
